From Vision to Handover

Our Structured Design Journey

01

Initial Consultation

We gather at our Tilakwadi studio or online to review your project blueprints, explore your design tastes, and understand spatial needs.

02

On-Site Measurement

Our technical engineers perform a physical site survey to document dimensions, inspect plumbing outlets, and verify walls for modular fittings.

03

Design Planning

Our creative draftsmen design efficient layout blueprints focused on optimal ergonomics, fluid traffic patterns, and lighting.

04

3D Visualization

Experience high-fidelity photorealistic 3D renders of your future home. Adjust color options and materials before cabinet construction begins.

05

Material Selection

Walk through our sample showroom catalog. Select laminates, quartz, acrylics, and structural hardware with hand-feel confidence.

06

Factory Manufacturing

Your modular wood units are precision manufactured on state-of-the-art machinery to guarantee dust-free edges and flat-pack excellence.

07

Site Installation

Our professional carpenters assemble, align, and test your cabinets, sliding tracks, and false ceiling details for seamless fitting.

08

Final Handover

A comprehensive clean-up, final quality-check audit, and keys handover. Welcome into your dream luxury home design!

Modern Living Solutions

Why Choose Modular Over Carpentry?

01

Smart Space Saving

Maximized height shelving, pull-out wire baskets, dynamic corner carousels, and slim dividers utilize every cubic inch of your room.

02

Precision Engineering

Factory machine edge-banding and automatic drill alignments prevent crooked hinges, cabinet gaps, and peeling laminate corners.

03

Easy Cleaning & Repair

Individually mounted cabinet carcasses allow you to swap specific doors, tracks, or shelves without tearing down the entire wood fixture.

04

Infinite Customization

Mix, match, and tailor drawer modules, internal cabinet organizers, and external wood finishes to fit your everyday storage routine.

05

Premium Glass & Acrylics

Achieve ultra-glossy glass doors, luxurious anti-scratch matte panels, or raw walnut grain patterns that carpentry cannot recreate.

06

10-Year Built to Last

Boiling-water-proof (BWP) commercial marine ply and rust-protected hardware guarantee decades of use in humid kitchen spaces.
